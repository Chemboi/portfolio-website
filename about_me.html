About Me

My name is Jonathan Gauvin-Audet. I'm 30 years old and I'm a full-time R&D researcher scientist with a Bachelor's degree in Chemistry and a Master's degree in Materials Chemistry, specializing in heterogeneous catalysis and silica nanotechnology. I have significant experience in both academic and industrial research environments, having managed numerous R&D projects that focus on green chemistry, nanomaterials, and process optimization.

In my role as a R&D Specialist, I lead innovative projects including the development of high-performance silica from recycled glass, the encapsulation of chemotherapeutic agents in nanovectors for drug delivery, and the creation of anti-corrosion coatings for various industries. Additionally, I have experience in scaling up reactions for CO2 conversion and analyzing its kinetics using advanced techniques like FTIR and microfluidic devices.
